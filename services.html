Our Services

Everything you need for complete healthcare — diagnostics, treatment, recovery and beyond.

Comprehensive Healthcare Under One Roof

Krishna Hospital offers a full spectrum of medical, surgical and diagnostic services. With 500+ beds, 120+ specialist doctors and state-of-the-art equipment, we ensure you receive the best care without travelling far.

Diagnostic Services

Pathology & Lab Tests

Complete blood count, biochemistry, microbiology, histopathology and more with same-day results.

Radiology & Imaging

Digital X-Ray, CT Scan, MRI, Ultrasound, Mammography and PET Scan facilities.

Cardiac Diagnostics

ECG, Echocardiography, Holter monitoring, Treadmill test and cardiac catheterisation.

Pulmonary Function Test

Spirometry, peak flow measurement and sleep study for respiratory disorders.

Treatment & Surgery

Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive surgery for gallbladder, appendix, hernia and gynaecological conditions.

Chemotherapy

Day-care and inpatient chemotherapy administration with expert oncology nursing support.

Emergency Surgery

Round-the-clock surgical emergency services with dedicated trauma operation theatres.

Maternity & Delivery

Natural delivery, C-section, painless delivery and high-risk pregnancy management.

Support & Rehabilitation

Physiotherapy

Post-surgical rehabilitation, sports injury recovery and musculoskeletal pain management.

Dietetics & Nutrition

Personalised diet plans for diabetes, obesity, renal disease and post-operative recovery.

Counselling Services

Psychological counselling for patients and families dealing with chronic illness or trauma.

Home Care Services

Post-discharge nursing, IV therapy, wound dressing and physiotherapy at your home.

Digital & Online Services

Online Appointments

Book OPD appointments with any specialist doctor from the comfort of your home, 24/7.

Digital Lab Reports

Download your lab reports and prescriptions instantly via the patient portal.

Online Payments

Pay your bills securely online via UPI, net banking, credit/debit cards or EMI.

Tele-Consultation

Video consultations with doctors for follow-ups, prescriptions and second opinions.
