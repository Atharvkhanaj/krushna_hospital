Our Story

Healing with Heart Since 1998

Krishna Hospital was founded in 1998 with a single, powerful vision: to provide world-class healthcare to the communities of rural Maharashtra. What began as a modest 40-bed facility has grown into a full-service multi-specialty hospital with over 500 beds and 120 specialist doctors.

Located in Urun Ishwarpur, we serve not just our town but also the broader Sangli district — bringing advanced diagnostics, surgical expertise and compassionate nursing care to patients who previously had to travel to Pune or Mumbai for treatment.

Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to the community that has trusted us for over two and a half decades.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

From humble beginnings to a leading multi-specialty hospital — our growth reflects the community's trust.

1998

Founded

Dr. Ramkrishna Patil established the hospital with 40 beds and a mission to serve rural Maharashtra.

2003

ICU & Emergency Wing

A dedicated Intensive Care Unit and 24/7 emergency department were inaugurated, tripling patient capacity.

2008

Multi-Specialty Expansion

Cardiology, Neurology and Orthopedics departments were added, bringing specialist care to the district.

2013

NABH Accreditation

Awarded the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals certification, affirming our commitment to quality and safety.

2018

Digital Transformation

Launched the Hospital Management System — online appointments, digital reports and patient portal.

2023

New OPD Block & Cancer Centre

State-of-the-art Outpatient Department and a dedicated Cancer Care Centre opened to serve more patients.
